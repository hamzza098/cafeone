Welcome to Café Sylhet



Welcome to our sanctuary of flavor and culture, where every sip tells a story of Bangladesh's rich heritage.
At Café Sylhet, we blend the warmth of Bangladeshi hospitality with the aromatic allure of freshly brewed coffee, delectable pastries and masterfully crafted drinks.
Step into our cozy haven, where the tantalizing aroma of our signature brews beckons you to embark on a journey through the bustling streets of Dhaka or the serene tea gardens of Sylhet.
From traditional masala chai to innovative twists on classic espresso drinks, each cup is a celebration of the vibrant flavors and traditions that define our homeland.
Join us as we invite you to experience the essence of Bangladesh, one sip at a time.
